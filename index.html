How it works

Trust-driven payment flow

From voice input and fraud scoring to offline execution and personalized advice, TrustiPay combines multiple intelligence layers into a single payment experience.

1

Capture

User input begins through touch or voice, with multilingual and accessibility-first interaction patterns.

2

Understand

Local speech processing and controlled reasoning interpret intent while a state machine ensures safe navigation.

3

Protect

AI-powered transaction surveillance assigns risk and triggers approval, verification, or blocking actions.

4

Complete

Payments finalize online or offline, then sync securely to maintain ledger integrity and continuity.

Core solution modules

One platform. Four strategic capabilities.

TrustiPay’s architecture combines secure transaction handling with inclusive experiences and actionable intelligence.

Voice Controlled Dynamic & Inclusive Payment Interface

Designed for users who struggle with visually heavy interfaces, combining safer guided flows with local voice execution.

AI-Powered Fraud Detection & Transaction Surveillance

Uses anomaly detection and multi-stage Random Forest scoring to reduce false positives while capturing suspicious behavior.

Resilient Money Movement & Offline Value Module

Supports tamper-evident local ledgers, double-spend resistance, and recovery syncing after connectivity is restored.

AI-Based User Behaviour Analytics

Predicts savings trends, financial health levels, and personalized recommendations using behavior-aware ML features.

Multilingual Accessibility

Expands digital payment adoption by reducing language barriers and enabling broader, more human-centered usability.

Resilient Trust Layer

Combines fraud defense, explainable workflows, voice safety, and offline survivability to increase confidence in every payment.

Technical highlights

Local-first voice pipeline

Microphone input, voice activity detection, speech-to-text, agent reasoning, and speech response work together for guided interactions.

State-machine-driven safety

LLMs assist understanding, while deterministic navigation logic protects sensitive payment tasks from unsafe execution paths.

Hybrid fraud decisioning

Risk scoring maps directly into actions such as approve, OTP verification, or block-and-alert outcomes.
